    Anatomy of the Tactile Bump

    The "bump" is created by the interaction between the stem's legs and the metal contact leaf. As the stem moves down, the non-linear shape of the leg forces the leaf to flex, creating that signature feedback.

    • Pre-travel: 2.0mm to bump
    • Actuation force: 55gf – 67gf
    • Total travel: 4.0mm
