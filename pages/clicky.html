    How Clicky Switches Work

    Unlike linear counterparts, clicky switches provide a definitive physical resistance followed by a drop, signaling exactly when the key registers.

    The click mechanism—either a sliding click jacket or a spring-metal click bar—produces both the tactile bump and the audible "click" simultaneously at the actuation point.
